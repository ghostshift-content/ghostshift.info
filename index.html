        dossier — subject: jay_p
      

    


    

      

        
I started breaking things legally in 2018, straight out of
          a Master's in Info Tech at Victoria University, and I haven't really stopped since.
          200+ pentests later — across banks, government, fintech and healthcare — I've seen one forgotten SSRF turn into domain-wide compromise through AWS SSM — no 0day required.

        
This site is my personal notebook. It's where I dump write-ups, CTF notes,
          half-finished exploit chains and the occasional opinion. Nothing here represents
          any employer, client, or platform I've ever worked with.

        
If something here helped you, or if I got something wrong — I'd genuinely
